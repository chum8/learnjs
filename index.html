Learning JavaScript

These lessons were completed while studying "A Smarter Way to Learn JavaScript" by Mark Myers, copyright 2013-2014. The exercises generally reproduce the coding examples in Myers' excellent book. I had some prior knowledge of JavaScript and worked through the book to fill in some knowledge gaps. At times, I modified the exercises slightly. I completed them in or around January, 2017.

--Douglas Singer
