This chart shows the structure of children in the DOM 
html: child of document
	head: first child of html
		title: child of head
			Simple document
		/title
	/head
	body: second child of html
		div: child of body
			p: first child of div
				text in here is child of 1st p
			/p
			p: second child of div
				text in here is child of 2nd p
			/p
			p em: child of em  /em /p
		/div
	/body
/html
